Allies

Overview

World War I was fought between July 28, 1914 to November 11, 1918. Casualty totals were in the millions. Soldiers lived in trenches while fighting starvation, disease, and death at the hands on new technologies. This war was fought on land, on the sea, below the sea, and in the air. The Allies were victorious against the Central Powers and Germany was blamed for the war in the Treaty of Versailles.

Central Powers

Germany, Austria-Hungary, Ottoman Empire, Bulgaria
